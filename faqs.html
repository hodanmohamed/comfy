Frequently Asked Questions

Need more answers? Send us an email or say Hello!

How long until we deliver your product

Our delivery times vary based on your location and the product. Typically, you can expect your order to arrive within 5-7 business days. For more precise delivery estimates, please check our Shipping Policy.

Where can I see your sizing chart?

You can easily find our sizing chart on our website under the 'Size Guide' section. We provide detailed measurements and guidelines to help you choose the perfect fit for your comfort and style.

Do you deliver to the United States?

Yes, we offer delivery to the United States. We provide nationwide shipping to ensure our customers in the U.S. can enjoy our products and exceptional service. For more details, please visit our Shipping Information page.

Can I exchange an item?

Absolutely, we offer a hassle-free exchange policy. If your purchase doesn't meet your expectations or needs, you can exchange it within 30 days of receipt. Visit our Returns and Exchanges page for more information on the process.

Is there a purchase order limit?

No, we don't have a purchase order limit. You're welcome to place orders according to your needs and preferences. We strive to accommodate all customers, whether it's a single item or a larger purchase.

Can I send a product as a gift?

Absolutely! You can select the 'Gift' option at checkout, and we'll ensure your order is beautifully wrapped and include a personalized message. Make someone's day special with a thoughtful gift from us.

Where can I use my discount code?

You can redeem your discount code during the checkout process on our website. Look for the 'Discount Code' or 'Promo Code' field and enter your code there to enjoy the savings on your purchase.

Can I change my address?

Yes, you can update your address before your order ships. Simply log into your account, go to 'My Account,' and select 'Edit Address.' If your order has already shipped, please contact our customer support for assistance.
